Kerala, known as "God's own country," is famous for its stunning natural beauty, with a unique mix of serene backwaters, lush hill stations, and beautiful beaches like Marari and Kovalam. Its popular destinations include Munnar and Wayanad, offering cool climates and scenic tea plantations, while enchanting waterfalls like Athirapally and peaceful rivers like the Periyar enhance the state's allure. With rich wildlife and captivating landscapes from the Western Ghats to the Arabian Sea, Kerala is a paradise for nature lovers and travelers seeking relaxation.
